Research on technology addiction

For my project, I want to explore the issue of technology addiction. In particular, I will address the built-in features in social networking sites (such as Facebook, Instagram, and Snapchat) and video platforms (such as YouTube and Netflix) that promote excessive use, as well as its consequences on mental health.

Screenshots from Netflix:

In terms of the look of my project, I want to model the design of Netflix's interface by:

Including large and simple titles

Displaying bold, full-screen images

Featuring mockups on mobile devices as a form of satire

Having a "Frequently Asked Questions" section, where I have a question-answer format to display part of my information
